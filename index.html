Welcome to Musical Shoope

Music stores arose to service the needs of the local community. This included not only individual amateur musicians, but schools from elementary to college level, civic bands and orchestras, churches, and entertainment ensembles that performed at events of the community and its organizations. Instruments might be purchased outright, leased or rented. Specific or non-stock items could be ordered through the store.
